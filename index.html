Healthy Pepperoni Pizza!

Ingredients

  • 1 flatbread (stonefire artisan flatbread is my favorite!)
  • 1 cup of reduced fat mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup of barilla marinara sauce
  • 15 slices of turkey pepperoni

Instructions

  1. Preheat over to 350 degrees
  2. Sread out wax paper on a large pan and drizzle extra virgin olive oil on top
  3. Place the flatbread pizza on top
  4. Leave flatbread pizza in oven for 10-12 minutes
  5. Finally Enjoy!
